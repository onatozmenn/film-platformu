## Big Buck Bunny

`Big Buck Bunny` (2008) is an explicitly licensed, non-fictional catalog entry produced by the Blender Foundation. The official [license statement](https://peach.blender.org/about/) applies Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 to the published project content and permits commercial redistribution with attribution. The canonical license deed is [CC BY 3.0 Unported](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/).

Required attribution: `(c) copyright 2008, Blender Foundation / www.bigbuckbunny.org`. The unmodified complete film must retain its full end-credit roll. The application does not claim Blender Foundation endorsement. Blender and Creative Commons logos, trademarks, and the excluded DVD cover/disc artwork are not included.

The logos and DVD artwork excluded by the project license are not used. The unbranded original film frame is reused with responsive cropping as both catalog art and backdrop; the pixels are otherwise unmodified.

The official 320×180 MP4 source is listed in the [Blender download directory](https://download.blender.org/peach/bigbuckbunny_movies/BigBuckBunny_320x180.mp4). The film binary is not committed or hotlinked by the application; production playback remains unavailable until an operator ingests that authorized source into Mux and attaches the resulting ready asset through the admin workflow.
